Burrito

Prep: 25 MINS

Cook: 10 MINS

Total: 35 HR

Cuisine: TEXMEX, Gluten Free, Vegan, Vegetarian

Serving size: 6 WITH RICE

INGREDIENTS:

  1. ▢ 1 cup Mexican rice
  2. ▢ 1 tomato (diced)
  3. ▢ 1 avocado (diced)
  4. ▢ 1/2 red onion, diced (raw or pan yellowed)
  5. 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds removed and finely diced (adjust to taste)
  6. ▢ 1 package Impossible Meat
  7. ▢ shredded/crumbled cheese of your showing
  8. 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  9. 1 cup corn
  10. 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  11. Juice of 1 lime

Ground Spices

  1. ▢salt
  2. ▢1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  3. ▢1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  4. ▢A generous amount of freshly cracked black pepper
  5. ▢paprike

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Make the rice. Duh.
  • Dice the tomato, avocado, onions, and jalapeños
  • Mash up Impossible Burger then fry in pan (to put in rice later)
  • Heat up black beans. While heating and stirring, mix in spices.
  • Heat up and add butter and spices to corn.
  • Mix everything in steps above together; or, alternatively, layer on top of one another.
  • Add chopped cilantro and lime juice on top.
