Garden Burrito Bowl

Prep: 25 MINS

Cook: 10 MINS

Total: 35 HR

Cuisine: TEXMEX, Gluten Free, Vegan, Vegetarian

Serving size: 6 WITH RICE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup Mexican rice
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • 1 avocado (diced)
  • 1/2 red onion, diced (raw or pan yellowed)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeds removed and finely diced (adjust to taste)
  • 1 package Impossible Meat
  • Shredded/crumbled cheese of your showing
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime

Ground Spices:

  • salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • A generous amount of freshly cracked black pepper
  • paprika

INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Make the rice. Duh.
  • Dice the tomato, avocado, onions, and jalapeños
  • Mash up Impossible Burger then fry in pan (to put in rice later)
  • Heat up black beans. While heating and stirring, mix in spices.
  • Heat up and add butter and spices to corn.
  • Mix everything in steps above together; or, alternatively, layer on top of one another.
  • Add chopped cilantro and lime juice on top.
