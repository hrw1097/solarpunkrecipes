Vegetarian Chili

Prep: 25 MINS

Cook: 35 MINS

Total: 1 HR

Cuisine: Southern-INSPIRED, Vegan, Vegetarian

INGREDIENTS:

    Ingredients:

  1. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  2. 1 large onion, chopped
  3. >3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  5. 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  6. 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  7. 2 carrots, diced
  8. 2 celery stalks, diced
  9. >1 zucchini, diced
  10. 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  11. 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  12. 1 can (15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  13. 1 can each (15 ounces) kidney, pinto, and black beans, drained and rinsed
  14. 2 tablespoons chili powder
  15. 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  16. 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  17. 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  18. Salt and black pepper to taste
  19. 4 cups vegetable broth
  20. 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  21. Grated cheddar cheese or vegan cheese (optional for topping)
  22. Sour cream or vegan sour cream (optional for topping)

    23. Ground Spices

  23. ▢1 ½ teaspoons curry powder
  24. ▢1 teaspoon ground coriander
  25. ▢½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  26. ▢1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  27. ▢¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  28. ▢A generous amount of freshly cracked black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

Instructions:

  • Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  • Add the chopped red, yellow, and green bell peppers, carrots, celery, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften.
  • Stir in the corn kernels, diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and pinto beans. Mix well.
  • Add the chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir to evenly distribute the spices among the vegetables and beans.
  • Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the chili simmer for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • If you're using TVP or cooked lentils for extra protein, add them to the chili and stir well. Let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  • Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Add more chili powder or cayenne pepper for extra heat, or more salt and pepper to suit your taste.
  • Serve the vegetarian chili hot in bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and, if desired, top with grated cheddar cheese or vegan cheese, a dollop of sour cream or vegan sour cream, and sliced green onions.
  • Enjoy your hearty and delicious vegetarian chili with some crusty bread or cornbread on the side!
