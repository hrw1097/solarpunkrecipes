Fruit Smoothie

Prep: 15 MINS

Blend: 2 MINS

Total: 17 MINS

Cuisine: Fruit, Gluten Free, Vegan, Vegetarian

Serving size: 1-2 people

INGREDIENTS:

  1. ▢ Apple
  2. ▢ Blueberry
  3. ▢ Strawberry
  4. ▢ Banana
  5. ▢ Plant Milk (coconut, soy, etc.)

Spices/Other

  1. ▢ Cinnamon
  2. ▢ Honey

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Make the rice. Duh.
  • Dice the tomato, avocado, onions, and jalapeños
  • Mash up Impossible Burger then fry in pan (to put in rice later)
  • Heat up black beans. While heating and stirring, mix in spices.
  • Heat up and add butter and spices to corn.
  • Mix everything in steps above together; or, alternatively, layer on top of one another.
  • Add chopped cilantro and lime juice on top.
